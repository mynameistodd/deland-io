WhoCalled  is the easiest way to instantly search on whocalled.us!   &nbsp;     This app only responds when the incoming phone number is NOT in your phonebook. After declining the unknown number, a notification will appear in your notification bar. Simply click the notification and it will search whocalled.us, showing the most popular answer!   Permission explanations:  READ_PHONE_STATE: to know when call is over.  INTERNET: to lookup who the caller is.  READ_CONTACTS: to filter out known contacts.
